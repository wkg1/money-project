## 5. Strategic Summary: Where to Focus

### The Winning Formula for May 2026:

```
NICHE-SPECIFIC + IMMEDIATELY USEFUL + PRICED $29-$49 = Highest probability of profit
```

### The "Do NOT Do" List:
1. **Do NOT** create generic "100 ChatGPT prompts" PDFs — market is dead, prices collapsed
2. **Do NOT** create generic weekly planners — competing against 1.6M+ listings on Etsy
3. **Do NOT** price below $9 — you need massive volume to make real money
4. **Do NOT** create a course as your first product (unless you already have an audience) — digital downloads outsell courses 2.5:1
5. **Do NOT** target "everyone" — the tighter your niche, the higher your conversion rate

### The "DO" List:
1. **DO** niche down relentlessly (ADHD Notion template, not "productivity template")
2. **DO** price in the $29–$49 sweet spot (highest revenue per product in Gumroad data)
3. **DO** create tools, not just information (downloads > courses by 2.5x sales volume)
4. **DO** bundle for higher average order value (bundle price = 3x single price)
5. **DO** sell on Gumroad first (lower fees than Etsy, direct customer relationship)
6. **DO** consider a subscription/membership layer as your second product (recurring revenue = stability)
