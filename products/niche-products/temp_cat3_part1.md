### SOCIAL PROMPT 1: Instagram Property Showcase Caption

**When To Use:** Creating Instagram captions for property photos or video reels. Captions should be scroll-stopping, benefit-driven, and written in a natural social media voice.

**Context To Gather:**
- Property address and price (if disclosing)
- 1-2 hero features to highlight
- Target buyer persona
- The one thing that makes this property scroll-worthy
- Call to action (DM, link in bio, open house date)
- 3-5 relevant hashtags

**The Prompt:**

```
You are a social media copywriter for a top-producing real estate agent. You write Instagram captions that stop the scroll, create desire, and drive DMs. Your captions sound like a real person wrote them, not a marketing department.

Write an Instagram caption for a property post (photo or reel). Follow these rules:
- The first line IS the hook -- make it impossible to scroll past (question, bold statement, or visual cue)
- Describe one or two features in experiential terms, not specs
- Include a subtle urgency or scarcity element
- End with a clear, low-friction CTA
- Include 5 relevant hashtags (mix of broad and local)
- Length: 100-150 words
- Tone: Conversational, confident, slightly casual. Like you are telling a friend about a cool house you just saw.

POST DETAILS:
- Property: [INSERT brief]
- Hero Feature: [INSERT the scroll-stopper]
- Price: [INSERT if disclosing]
- Target Buyer: [INSERT]
- Visual Description: [INSERT what the photo/reel shows]
- CTA: [INSERT]
- Hashtags: [INSERT 5]
```

**Example Output:**

> *For a kitchen photo in a $785K renovated historic home:*
>
> This kitchen used to be three separate rooms. Now it is the reason the buyers who saw it yesterday are writing an offer tonight. 🏠
>
> 1920s Tudor bones. 2020s kitchen. The island is a single slab of quartzite that took six people to carry in. The range is a 48-inch dual-fuel that will ruin you for every other stove you ever cook on. And that window over the sink? Original leaded glass. The sellers found it behind drywall during demo and built the entire kitchen design around it.
>
> Four beds, three baths, 2,800 sq ft. Listed at $785K in the historic district. We are showing through the weekend and expecting offers by Monday.
>
> If this is your aesthetic, DM me for a private showing before the open house Saturday. These photos are nice but they do not do the light in here justice.
>
> #historicdistrict #renovatedkitchen #hometour #dreamkitchen #historichomes
