Listing Descriptions
Luxury Waterfront Property Description
You are a luxury real estate copywriter. Write a 120-word listing description for a waterfront home with [key features]. Use sensory language. Evoke lifestyle, not just square footage. Target: luxury buyers earning $500k+. Tone: sophisticated but warm.
Sample Output
"Wake up to the sound of gentle waves and golden sunrises painting the water from your private 80-foot shoreline. This 4,200 sq. ft. coastal retreat blends timeless Craftsman architecture with modern indulgence — floor-to-ceiling glass, a chef's kitchen with Wolf & Sub-Zero appliances, and a primary suite that feels like a five-star resort..."
Email Follow-Up
Open House Lead Nurture Sequence
Create a 3-email follow-up sequence for open house visitors who showed interest but weren't ready to commit. Email 1: value-add (24h later). Email 2: social proof (3 days later). Email 3: gentle urgency (5 days later). Keep each under 100 words. Friendly, zero pressure.
Sample Output
Email 1: "Hi Sarah — great meeting you at the Madison Ave open house on Saturday. I put together a quick market snapshot for that neighborhood — prices, days-on-market trends, and what similar homes have sold for this quarter. No pressure at all, just thought you'd find it useful..."
Social Media
Instagram Reel Script for Just Listed
Write a 30-second Instagram Reel script to promote a newly listed home. Hook in the first 3 seconds. Highlight 3 standout features. End with a clear CTA. Write the on-screen text captions and voiceover. Use a fast-paced, modern style.
Sample Output
Hook (0-3s): "This kitchen just made every home chef in Austin jealous..." Features: Calacatta marble island, hidden walk-in pantry, 48" gas range. CTA: "First open house this Saturday — link in bio to RSVP."
